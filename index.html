My current favorite foundation is the "Velvet Matte Skin Tint Broad Spectrum SPF 30" by Nars cosmetics. This foundation retails for $45. It comes in a variety of shades. The color that matches me is called Cuzco. Although this foundation is a skin tint, it acts as a foundation. It gives low to medium coverage and can be buildable to full coverage. The reason why I love this foundation so much is because it feels very light on the skin. Almost as if no foundation was applied. I also love that this foundation is long wearing. I notice that when I go to events, the foundation will not fade at all. Overall, it's a great pick.

I have been really loving my go to look at the moment. When I am in the mood to put on makeup but I do not want a full face of makeup, I will do the following. I will fill in my eyebrows with a dipbrow from Anastasia Beverly Hills. I will fill them in with slight strokes. I then use concealor in my T-zone area: the forehead, under my eyes, the bridge of my nose, and finally my chin. I then will a little bronzer and highlight. Finally, I will apply some mascara, lipstick and powder the rest of my face with a translucent powder to set the concealor in place. This is my routine for natural every-day makeup.
