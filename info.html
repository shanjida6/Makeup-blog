Although makeup is fun and games. Sometimes we have to look at the problems with the beauty industry. One important topic that is not talked enough about is animal testing and animal cruelty when creating makeup products. I believe animal cruelty is an important topic everyone should be aware of when considering to buy products. Many popular brands today continue to use animal testing and we should stop buying makeup from these brands in order to influence them to find alternatives to animal testing.
